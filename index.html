What We Do

Concrete coating & flooring services

One crew for every concrete surface — surface prep, coating, and finish — for homes and businesses across the greater Charlotte area.

Epoxy Flake Flooring

Tough, slip-resistant flake systems for garages, basements and living spaces — they shrug off hot tires, oil and salt, and wipe clean in seconds.

Shop Floors

Durable, low-maintenance floors for workshops, warehouses and commercial spaces — built to take heavy use and stay looking sharp.

Metallic

One-of-a-kind marbled, three-dimensional metallic epoxy floors. No two are ever the same — a true statement piece.

Concrete Stain

Rich, translucent color that soaks into the slab for a natural, polished stone look — indoors or out.

Overlay

Resurface tired concrete — patios, porches, pool decks, driveways and walkways — with a fresh, textured, decorative topping built to last.

Our Story

We treat your floor like our own

What started as a family passion for doing concrete the right way has grown into a trusted local name for epoxy and stained floors.

For over four years, Tom Miller and his family crew have been transforming garages, basements, patios and businesses across the region. We handle the entire process in-house — from the first inspection to the final coat — so you always know exactly who is working on your home.

We take pride in clean job sites, honest pricing, and standing behind every floor — 10 years on residential work, 1–5 years on commercial depending on the flooring system installed. When the job is done, we want you to be as proud of your floor as we are.
